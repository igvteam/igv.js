About this view

  • Ideogram Track: Shows chromosome cytobands with red viewport indicator
  • DNA Sequence Track: Displays nucleotide sequence at base-pair resolution
  • RefSeq Genes: Gene annotations from NCBI RefSeq database
  • BigWig Track: Example signal track from ENCODE project
  • Gene Search: Enter a gene name (e.g., EGFR, TP53, BRCA1) or coordinates (chr:start-end)
