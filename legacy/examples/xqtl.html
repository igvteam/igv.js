QTL example

Example illustrates interaction with xqtl tracks. To highlight eqtls and associated phenotypes (genes) use the "Search for..." option in the track menu. For example, from the "B cell eQTL" track:

  • Search for the snp rs5751734. Two eqtls and associated genes are highlighted for this snp
  • Search for the gene SMARCB1. The gene and associated eqtls are highlighted.
